02 / APPROACH

Technology is most interesting when it becomes a toy.

01

Keep the idea clear.

A project should be understandable in one sentence, even when the system beneath it is complex.

02

Use limits as material.

Ten kilobytes, live footage, real weather—constraints can give a project the character it needs.

03

Ship the experiment.

An unusual idea becomes meaningful when someone else can touch it, play it, and respond to it.
